Peru · Data & Transparency

Citizen dashboards built on official public data.

Peru State-Owned Enterprises Observatory 🏛️
Transparency for Peru's state companies (FONAFE + regional water utilities): KPIs, finances, real procurement (OCDS/OECE), rankings, diagnosis and recommendations. Static dashboard + versioned ETL.
✨ newReactEChartsPythonOCDS
Defense & Interior Sector Observatory 🛡️
Financial intelligence on Peru's Defense & Interior sector: state companies (SIMA, FAME, SEMAN), budget by agency and investment projects, pensions (CPMP) and pay scales by rank. Real, sourced data; unpublished figures flagged as estimates. Includes an AI assistant.
✨ newReactEChartsPythonGemini AI
Peru Economic Power Observatory 🕸️
Who controls what? Peru's economic groups, companies, shareholders and boards rebuilt as a graph from public data (SMV · BVL · SBS): PageRank, Louvain communities, an Economic Power Index (0–100), network affinity and conflict-of-interest detection. Static dashboard + versioned ETL.
✨ newReactCytoscapeEChartsPythonNetworkX
Peru Transparente 🇵🇪
A national map of public officials, government entities, state-owned companies and power networks: a unified database, a national power graph and dashboards built from public data (PTE, Comptroller, FONAFE, OCDS, SERVIR…). Source-traceable data, anti-overclaiming by design.
✨ newReactPythonFastAPIDatos abiertos
Observatorio Ambiental Peruano 🌳
Tablero de derrames de petróleo, deforestación y áreas naturales protegidas del Perú, con datos oficiales y procedencia marcada (verificado/estimado/referencial). Mapa nacional Leaflet y hoja de ruta en 3 fases.
✨ newReactViteLeafletPython
Observatorio de Universidades del Perú 🎓
Análisis bibliométrico de 95 universidades peruanas con OpenAlex + MEF + INDECOPI: índice ponderado por impacto, indicador ARWU (Nature & Science), filtros anti-predadoras y reclasificación contra el padrón MEF. Metodología depurada y trazable.
✨ newOpenAlexChart.jsLeafletPython
Plan Perú 2050 🇵🇪
Tablero navegable de las ~64 Comisiones Temáticas del CNPP/CIP: objetivos, alineamiento y prospectiva del planeamiento estratégico nacional al 2050.
✨ newJavaScriptGitHub Pages
Proyecto INTI — Digital Twin of Peru 2075 🌞
Territorial intelligence system for Peru's 1,891 districts with real data (HDI 2019, poverty and population from the 2017 INEI Census): 12 indices, diagnosis, 2075 foresight, national Leaflet map, district ranking, Top 50 projects, AI engine (OpenRouter) and municipal plans in PDF.
✨ newVanilla JSChart.jsLeaflet
Petroperú Analytics 🛢️
Public dashboard tracking Petroperú's financial, operational and corporate-governance evolution (20 years): 16 financial ratios, 5 boards, an interactive governance graph, a "play the director" simulator to turn the company around, and a Bloomberg-style day/night mode. Static SPA; seed data flagged as illustrative until audited financial statements are loaded.
✨ newReactTypeScriptEChartsPython
Congreso Abierto Peru 🏛️
Open-data observatory of the Congreso de la República: 14,704 bills, 3,826 laws, 59 committees and 11 years of real budget (S/625M→S/1,769M) scraped from the MEF. Dynamic year filter, light/dark mode and shareable cards.
✨ newPythonPlaywrightChart.js
Peru's Risks & Resilience 🇵🇪
Citizen dashboard on disasters and preparedness: earthquakes, tsunamis, El Niño, landslides, cold spells, deforestation, pandemics and global crises. Includes 6 interactive simulators (seismic, tsunami, SEIR pandemic, blackout, food crisis, El Niño). Data from IGP/SENAMHI/INDECI/MINAM/CENEPRED.
✨ newVanilla JSLeafletChart.js
Peru's Public Finances 🇵🇪
Interactive dashboard with 35 years (1990-2026) of data: GDP, public debt, budget, execution by region and by ministry portfolio, SUNAT tax revenue, mining and foreign trade. Official data from BCRP/MEF/INEI.
✨ liveReactD3Recharts
Peru's Education X-Ray 🇵🇪
Peruvian education-assessment dashboard — ENLA 2024 results, comparison by region/district, urban-rural gaps, reading vs mathematics. Data from MINEDU/UMC.
